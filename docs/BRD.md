# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

## Business Objective
To build an Expense & Budget Management System that enables users to track expenses, manage budgets, and analyze spending behavior.

## In Scope
- Expense entry and categorization
- Monthly expense reporting
- Budget monitoring

## Out of Scope
- Bank integrations
- Tax calculations

## Business Requirements
- Users should be able to record expenses
- Users should be able to view monthly summaries
- System should notify users when budgets are exceeded
